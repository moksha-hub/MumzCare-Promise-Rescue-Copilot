# Mumzworld Promise Rescue Policy Notes

These notes are a compact, citation-friendly policy fixture for the prototype.
They are grounded in public Mumzworld shipping, FAQ, returns, and contact pages,
but are not a scrape of product or retailer catalog pages.

Sources:
- https://www.mumzworld.com/en/shipping-rates
- https://www.mumzworld.com/en/faq
- https://www.mumzworld.com/en/returns-policy
- https://www.mumzworld.com/en/contact-us

## Same-Day And Yalla Delivery
Mumzworld publicly presents same-day delivery for key UAE cities such as Dubai,
Sharjah, and Abu Dhabi. KSA Yalla delivery is presented as same-day, while
non-Yalla KSA delivery can take several days. When a same-day promise has passed
and the carrier ETA is missing, agents should escalate for a verified update
instead of promising a new exact delivery time.

## Standard Delivery Windows
The FAQ describes broader delivery windows: UAE orders can take about 2-3 working
days, GCC orders about 5-10 working days, and international orders about 7-12
working days. A support reply should distinguish same-day commitments from these
broader windows.

## Return Pickup Windows
Eligible returns are generally handled within a 14-day return window from
delivery. Mumzworld says it arranges pickup for eligible returns. FAQ timing
indicates UAE pickup usually takes 1-3 business days and KSA pickup usually takes
2-5 business days depending on area.

## Refund Timing And Payment Method
Refund processing begins after courier collection and review. Wallet refunds can
be faster, while card refunds can take several business days. Mada, Tabby, and
Tamara can take longer. COD refunds are handled through Mumzworld Wallet, so an
agent should not promise cash refund to the original cash method.

## Damaged Wrong Or Missing Items
Damaged, defective, wrong, or missing items require evidence review and order
verification. The safe first action is replacement, exchange, pickup, or human
escalation depending on product category, stock, and proof. The copilot must not
claim the replacement or refund has been approved unless a tool confirms it.

## Delivered But Not Received
If tracking shows delivered but the customer says the order was not received,
the safe action is delivery investigation. Agents should avoid blaming the
customer or promising replacement/refund until delivery evidence is checked.

## Stock Cancellation
When an item becomes unavailable after order placement, the safe recovery options
are approved substitution, original-method refund when supported, or human
escalation. For urgent baby essentials, the case should be prioritized.

## Customer Communication Safety
Support replies should be empathetic and specific, but must not invent ETA,
refund approval, compensation, courier confirmation, or policy exceptions. If
facts are missing or contradictory, the correct answer is to say what is unknown
and route the case to human review.

## Arabic Reply Quality
Arabic support replies should be written natively for UAE/GCC ecommerce
customers. They should use clear Modern Standard Arabic, preserve verified facts,
and avoid word-for-word translation from English. The Arabic copy must not add
promises that are absent from the verified facts.
